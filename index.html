Ms. Pac-Man

Ms. Pac-Man: A demonstration of employing genetic programming for decision support of Pac-Man and the ghosts.

Ms. Pac-Man is searching for pills while trying to avoid the ghosts. The ghosts however try to find and eat Ms. Pac-Man.

Random Search:
both Ms. Pac-Man and the ghosts use random search
Genetic Programming Search:
Ms. Pac-Man uses genetic programming. The ghosts use random search.
Competitive Coevolutionary Search:
Both Ms. Pac-Man and the ghosts use genetic programming in a competitive coevolution.
